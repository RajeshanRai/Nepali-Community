Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you may want to know before completing your donation.

Is my donation secure?

Yes. Both Interac e-Transfer and card payments are encrypted and secure. Your personal information is protected and never shared with third parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Interac e-Transfer and credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard). Choose the payment method that works best for you.

Can I donate monthly?

Yes. Check "Make this a monthly recurring donation" and your contribution can continue monthly. For card donations, Stripe handles recurring billing securely.

Do I get a receipt?

Yes. Once your donation is confirmed, we send a detailed donation receipt by email, including your reference information.

Is my card information safe?

Absolutely. We use Stripe-hosted checkout for card payments, and full card details are not stored on our servers.

How will my donation help?

Your donation supports scholarships, cultural programs, community events, youth initiatives, and services for Nepali families in Vancouver.

Can my donation be anonymous?

Yes. Choose the anonymous option and your name will stay private in public donor mentions while still supporting the community.
