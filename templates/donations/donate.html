Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation secure?

Yes! Both Interact e-Transfer and card payments are encrypted and secure. Your personal information is protected and never shared with third parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Interact e-Transfer and credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard). Choose the payment method that works best for you.

Can I donate monthly?

Yes! Check the "Make this a monthly recurring donation" option, and we'll send you a reminder each month or charge your card automatically (for card payments).

Do I get a tax receipt?

Yes! Once your donation is confirmed, we'll email you an official tax receipt. Ensure you're registered with Canada Revenue Agency.

Is my card information safe?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption for card payments. We never store your full card details on our servers.

How will my donation help?

Your donation supports scholarships, cultural programs, community events, youth initiatives, and services for Nepali families.

Can my donation be anonymous?

Yes! Check the anonymous option and your name won't appear in our public donor list while still supporting the community.
